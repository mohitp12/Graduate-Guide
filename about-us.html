About Website

Grade Guide is a project meant for college students for personalized guidance. Graduate and undergraduate students especially, struggle to maintain or reach their dream grades, because apart from studies they have a big list of activities to be done. In such frenzy of activities, they tend to find it hard to understand which course/s require more attention at any particular time. So our project will provide a means for the students where they can compare their performance to other students in their class and aim at better grades.
